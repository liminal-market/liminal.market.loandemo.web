How do you liquidate?

Liquitys monitors the prices on your stocks and ETFs. If you go below the margin call percentage of 110%, Liquitys will liqudate your stocks and ETFs. This is the order of execution

  1. Liquitys monitors the price
  2. Liquitys notices that you have a margin call
  3. Liquitys calls Liminal.market smart contract to liquidate your equity
  4. Liminal.market sell your stocks and ETFs as quickly as possible (within seconds)
  5. Liminal.market funds Liquitys broker account with fiat USD dollars.
  6. Liquitys can transfer the money to exchange where it's traded for USDS
  7. USDS is burned
